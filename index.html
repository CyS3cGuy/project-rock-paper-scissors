Rock, Paper, Scissors, Minus One

Rock, Paper, Scissors Minus One is a simplified version of the classic game where one of the three traditional moves (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) is excluded, leaving players with only two choices. This adjustment alters the strategy and eliminates certain outcomes, such as draws in some scenarios.
